About School

Founder & Former MLA Parshurambhau Nandihalli: Mr. Parshurambhau Nandihalli was born on 7 August 1930 at Yellur, Karnataka. His father's name is Bharmaji , Mother's name Ningubai. Parshurambhau Nandihalli is a founder of a renowned school "Shivaji Vidhyalaya" in his community. Shivaji Vidhyalaya was the first school in yellur. Parshurambhau Nandihalli is a prominent figure in the field of education, having founded a highly successful school that continues to impact students' lives today. The name Parshurambhau Nandihalli is synonymous with excellence in education, thanks to his visionary leadership as the founder of a renowned school.

Head Master -M. B. Bachikar

M. B. Bachikar sir has been leading our school with great dedication and passion for 25 years. Bachikar sir brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to our school.His commitments to help poor people and student has helped to create a positive learning environment that fosters academic success and personal growth.He is highly approachable and values open communication with parents, students, and staff.His vision for school is to provide Quality Education to students and to develop the school in all circumtances. He is a social science teacher who worked as RP for last 20 years.Along with schools activities He is a Foram President for 2 years at taluka level.He has won the National builder award 2018 from Rotary club venugram Belgaum.
